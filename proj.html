  • Usable Privacy Project: A privacy policy of a website is an important document which should be read by any user of the website. Yet because of the sheer length and the legal terminologies present in a policy, it is seldom read. As a result, people are not aware of what the websites could do with their personal data. We are trying to fix this problem. As a part of the Usable Privacy Policy Project, we are trying to build an intelligent information system capable of understanding policies and also summarizing the key important features and presenting it to the user. To start this effort, we are in need of a set of policies which are carefully annotated by people who understand the importance of this project. Here's your chance to make the Internet a safer place!. Use our crowd sourcing app and annotate some privacy policies!
